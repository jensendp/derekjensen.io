First AI Project: Build a Simple Tool (Step-by-Step Guide)
85% of AI projects fail, usually from picking problems too big.
1
Pick a one-sentence problem you personally experience this week.
2
Start building tomorrow, not researching today—give yourself hard deadlines.
3
Skip frameworks and comparison videos; open an AI tool first.
4
Build small and useful before attempting big and impressive features.
