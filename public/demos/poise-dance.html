Our Story

Rooted in Purpose,
Built on Values.

Poise Dance was founded with a simple but powerful belief: that dance education should build character alongside technique. Our studio is a place where students learn to move — and learn to live — with intention.

Every instructor at Poise is selected not just for their dance expertise, but for their ability to be a mentor, a role model, and a motivator for young people. We're not just teaching steps — we're shaping futures.

From our youngest Mini Movers to our most advanced company dancers, every student is seen, celebrated, and challenged to become the best version of themselves.
