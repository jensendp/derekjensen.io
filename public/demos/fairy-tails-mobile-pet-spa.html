Our Story

More Than Grooming —
It's Pure Love

Fairy Tails Mobile Pet Spa was born from a simple belief: your pet deserves a calm, loving, and stress-free grooming experience — without the chaos of a traditional salon.

With over 15 years of award-winning grooming experience, our certified groomer comes directly to your home or business in a fully self-sufficient, climate-controlled mobile spa. We bring everything — no need for your water or electricity.

Every appointment is one-on-one. No cages. No other animals present. No distractions. Just your beloved pet receiving the royal treatment they deserve, in the comfort and safety of their own neighborhood.
